# Dr. Keith Ndlovu Website - Project Overview

## Project Vision
Create a sophisticated, professional website that showcases Dr. Keith Ndlovu as both a respected medical professional and accomplished artist, while highlighting his leadership role at Westpoint Medical Centre and artistic endeavors through Artistico Studios.

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Art collectors and enthusiasts
- **Secondary**: Medical professionals and patients
- **Tertiary**: Business partners and collaborators

## Key Objectives
1. **Establish Professional Credibility**: Showcase dual expertise in medicine and art
2. **Art Portfolio Display**: Create an elegant gallery for artwork showcase
3. **Medical Practice Promotion**: Highlight Westpoint Medical Centre
4. **Art Business Integration**: Feature Artistico Studios services
5. **Lead Generation**: Convert visitors into art buyers or medical patients

## Website Inspiration Analysis (Larson Studio)
**Key Elements to Emulate:**
- **Minimalist Design**: Clean, uncluttered layout focusing on artwork
- **High-Quality Imagery**: Professional photography with consistent lighting
- **Grid-Based Gallery**: Systematic artwork presentation
- **Artist Biography**: Comprehensive background and story
- **Categorized Content**: Organized by medium/type
- **Professional Navigation**: Clear, intuitive menu structure
- **Responsive Design**: Works seamlessly across devices

## Technology Stack
- **Framework**: Next.js 14 (App Router)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Components**: shadcn/ui
- **Typography**: Custom font selection for artistic feel
- **Images**: Next.js Image optimization
- **Forms**: React Hook Form with validation
- **Animation**: Framer Motion (subtle animations)

## Success Metrics
- Art inquiries/sales conversions
- Medical consultation bookings
- Time spent on gallery pages
- Contact form submissions
- Social media engagement
