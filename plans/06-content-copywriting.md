# Content Creation & Copywriting Plan

## Brand Voice & Tone

### Voice Characteristics
- **Authoritative yet Approachable**: Professional expertise without intimidation
- **Warm and Human**: Personal connection despite high-level achievements
- **Sophisticated**: Cultured, educated, internationally minded
- **Inspiring**: Motivational story of dual success
- **Trustworthy**: Medical credibility with artistic authenticity

### Tone Variations by Section

#### Medical Practice Content
- **Professional**: Clinical accuracy, medical terminology when appropriate
- **Reassuring**: Patient comfort and confidence building
- **Clear**: Accessible explanations of complex medical concepts
- **Compassionate**: Empathetic understanding of patient concerns

#### Art Portfolio Content
- **Passionate**: Emotional connection to creative work
- **Descriptive**: Rich, sensory language about artistic processes
- **Reflective**: Thoughtful insights into creative inspiration
- **Accessible**: Art appreciation without pretension

#### About/Biography Content
- **Personal**: Authentic storytelling, vulnerable moments
- **Ambitious**: Drive and determination narrative
- **Humble**: Gratitude and acknowledgment of support
- **Visionary**: Forward-thinking perspective on both fields

- **Alt Text**: Descriptive, keyword-rich image descriptions
- **Internal Linking**: Connect related medical and artistic content
- **External Linking**: Credible medical and art sources
