## User Journey Mapping

### Art Collector Journey
1. **Discovery**: Homepage hero → Art Portfolio
2. **Exploration**: Browse categories → Individual artwork pages
3. **Interest**: Read artist background → View similar works
4. **Decision**: Contact for pricing → Commission inquiry
5. **Purchase**: Secure transaction → Delivery coordination

### Medical Patient Journey
1. **Research**: Homepage medical credentials → About section
2. **Verification**: Medical career → Westpoint Medical Centre
3. **Information**: Services → Patient information
4. **Booking**: Contact → Appointment scheduling
5. **Preparation**: Patient forms → Visit confirmation

### Business Partner Journey
1. **Introduction**: Homepage → About biography
2. **Credibility**: Both medical and artistic sections
3. **Opportunity**: Artistico Studios → Services
4. **Collaboration**: Contact → Partnership discussion
5. **Agreement**: Terms → Project initiation
