# Brand Identity & Visual Design System

## Brand Positioning
**"Where Medicine Meets Art"** - Dr. Keith Ndlovu represents the intersection of healing and creativity, combining scientific precision with artistic vision.

## Brand Personality
- **Sophisticated**: Elevated, professional, cultured
- **Trustworthy**: Medical expertise, reliable healthcare
- **Creative**: Artistic vision, innovative thinking
- **Approachable**: Warm, human connection
- **International**: Global perspective, diverse influences

## Typography System

### Primary Font: "Inter"
- **Usage**: Body text, navigation, UI elements
- **Characteristics**: Clean, professional, highly readable
- **Weights**: 300, 400, 500, 600, 700

### Secondary Font: "Playfair Display"
- **Usage**: Headings, artistic descriptions, quotes
- **Characteristics**: Elegant, artistic, sophisticated
- **Weights**: 400, 500, 600, 700

### Accent Font: "Source Code Pro"
- **Usage**: Technical details, credentials, captions
- **Characteristics**: Modern, precise, clinical

## Visual Hierarchy

### Heading Scale
```css
h1: 3.5rem (56px)  - Hero titles, main page headers
h2: 2.5rem (40px)  - Section headers
h3: 1.875rem (30px) - Subsection headers
h4: 1.5rem (24px)  - Card titles, artwork titles
h5: 1.25rem (20px) - Small headings
h6: 1rem (16px)    - Labels, metadata
```

### Body Text
```css
body-large: 1.125rem (18px) - Introduction text, important content
body: 1rem (16px)           - Standard body text
body-small: 0.875rem (14px) - Captions, metadata
body-xs: 0.75rem (12px)     - Fine print, copyright
```

## Component Style Guidelines

### Cards
- **Shadow**: Subtle drop shadow for depth
- **Border**: 1px solid neutral-light
- **Radius**: 8px for soft, professional feel
- **Padding**: 24px standard, 16px mobile

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Navy background, white text, gold hover
- **Secondary**: White background, navy border, navy text
- **Ghost**: Transparent background, navy text, gold underline on hover

### Images
- **Aspect Ratios**: 
  - Hero images: 16:9
  - Artwork thumbnails: 4:5 (portrait) or 5:4 (landscape)
  - Profile photos: 1:1
- **Border Radius**: 4px for images, 50% for profile photos
- **Hover Effect**: Subtle scale (1.02x) with opacity overlay

### Navigation
- **Style**: Horizontal, centered
- **Hover**: Gold underline animation
- **Active**: Gold color with bold weight
- **Mobile**: Slide-out drawer with overlay

## Logo Concepts

### Primary Logo
- **Style**: Text-based with medical cross and paintbrush elements
- **Layout**: "Dr. Keith Ndlovu" with subtitle "Medicine • Art • Leadership"
- **Colors**: Navy blue with gold accents

### Favicon
- **Design**: Stylized combination of stethoscope and paintbrush
- **Style**: Minimalist, recognizable at small sizes

## Photography Guidelines

### Artwork Photography
- **Background**: Pure white or neutral cream
- **Lighting**: Even, soft lighting to avoid harsh shadows
- **Angle**: Straight-on, minimal distortion
- **Resolution**: High-res for zoom functionality
- **Consistency**: Same lighting setup for all pieces

### Portrait Photography
- **Style**: Professional but warm, natural lighting
- **Setting**: Medical office or art studio
- **Attire**: Professional medical attire OR artistic casual wear
- **Expression**: Confident, approachable, thoughtful

### Environmental Photography
- **Medical**: Clean, modern medical facility shots
- **Artistic**: Studio workspace, art creation process
- **Combined**: Shots showing both sides of his practice
